Tournament stats — chart examples

One rendered example of every chart proposed in docs/tournament-stats-design.md §4. The goal here is to see the shape of the data each chart consumes — not styling, colors, or app design. Every chart uses mock data modeled on the real data shapes read out of the stats/tournament layer (standings/parts JSON, the ChartBundle fields, game_player_turn/player_summaries, and the parser blob types). Numbers are invented; structure is faithful.
